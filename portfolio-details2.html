FitMantra - AI-Based Virtual Gym Trainer

Overview

The Smart Inventory and Navigation Suite for Walmart is an integrated system aimed at optimizing supply chain efficiency and improving customer experience. The project features automated video-based dispatch tracking, AR-guided in-store navigation, and real-time inventory management integrated with an alert system for seamless operations.

Key Features

  • Video Processing: Real-time tracking ensures precise product dispatch counts, reducing errors in warehouse operations.
  • In-Store Navigation: Augmented Reality (AR)-based guidance enhances shopping efficiency, allowing customers to locate products quickly in large retail stores.
  • Smart Inventory Management: Combines AI/ML and real-time alerts to maintain efficient stock control, minimizing discrepancies and ensuring timely replenishments.

Technologies Used

  • Video Processing: OpenCV, TensorFlow
  • AR-Based Navigation: Unity, ARCore
  • Backend Integration: Flask
  • Programming Languages: Python, JavaScript, HTML/CSS

Outcome

  • Operational Efficiency: Significantly reduced stock discrepancies through integrated tracking and inventory systems.
  • Enhanced Customer Experience: Intuitive AR navigation system enabled faster product discovery in large retail environments, leading to improved customer satisfaction.
