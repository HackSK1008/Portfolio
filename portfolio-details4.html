Demand Forcasting for E- commerce

Overview

The Demand Forecasting for E-Commerce project was developed during an internship at Infosys to enhance operational efficiency for online retail platforms. The solution focuses on accurately predicting customer demand using advanced data analysis and machine learning techniques, enabling e-commerce businesses to optimize inventory management, reduce operational costs, and meet customer demands effectively.

Key Features

  • Advanced Demand Prediction: Utilized machine learning algorithms to forecast demand patterns across various product categories and geographies.
  • Data Preprocessing:Cleaned datasets by handling missing values and outliers, ensuring high data quality for reliable forecasts.
  • Model Optimization: Applied multiple time series models and hyperparameter tuning to achieve maximum prediction accuracy.
  • Visualization and Insights: Delivered actionable insights through interactive dashboards and visual reports to support decision-making.
  • Scalable Solution: Designed the forecasting system to handle large datasets and adapt to evolving e-commerce trends.

Technologies Used

  • Data Processing: Python (Pandas, NumPy), SQL.
  • Machine Learning: Time series analysis using ARIMA, Prophet, and XGBoost.
  • Visualization Tools: Matplotlib, Seaborn, Power BI for generating actionable insights.
  • Development Environment: Google Colab

Outcome

  • Operational Efficiency: Improved inventory planning, reducing stockouts and overstocking by over 20%.
  • Enhanced Accuracy: Achieved high prediction accuracy through model validation and optimization, aligning supply with demand.
  • Business Impact: Enabled the client to reduce operational costs and improve customer satisfaction with timely product availability.

Awards & Recognition

  • Recognized as a Best Practice Project during the Infosys Springboard Internship for its innovation and real-world applicability.
