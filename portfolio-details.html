FitMantra - AI-Based Virtual Gym Trainer

Overview

FitMantra is a social impact project offering affordable AI-driven fitness solutions. It addresses challenges like improper form, lack of guidance, and motivation barriers.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Posture Correction: Minimizes injuries with computer vision algorithms.
  • Affordable Personal Trainer Alternative: Accessible fitness guidance for all.
  • Motivation and Engagement: Gamification and social features to keep users motivated.

Technologies Used

  • Python, C++, OpenCV, Mediapipe, Flutter, IoT

Outcome

  • Provided a cost-effective fitness solution for middle-class gym-goers.
  • Significantly improved workout effectiveness and safety.

Awards & Recognition

  • Recognition: Awarded 1st Prize in the Healthcare Domain at the International Hackathon organized by PICT Pune, showcasing innovation in leveraging AI for fitness.
