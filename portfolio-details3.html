Saheli - AI-Powered Women's Safety Plattform

Overview

Saheli is an AI-driven women’s safety application designed to provide discreet and real-time assistance during emergencies. It addresses critical safety challenges by enabling voice-activated emergency responses, ensuring help is accessible even when physical interaction with the phone is not feasible. Saheli leverages advanced features such as fake shutdown, proximity-based alerts, and a dedicated police dashboard to enhance safety measures for women in transit and logistics.

Key Features

  • Voice-Activated SOS: Detects the word "help" at a specific amplitude, triggering alerts without the need for physical phone interaction.
  • Proximity-Based Alerts: Notifies the top 5 contacts based on geographical proximity to ensure rapid response.
  • Fake Shutdown: Simulates phone shutdown while keeping critical features like location tracking and alerts operational in the background.
  • Fake Call Button: Allows users to simulate fake calls in unsafe situations while discreetly recording key details like vehicle information.
  • Police Dashboard: A backend system enabling real-time crime monitoring, alert management, crime zone mapping, and automated reporting.

Technologies Used

  • Voice Detection: Machine learning-based amplitude recognition.
  • Proximity Alerts: Geolocation services and cloud-based backend.
  • Crime Monitoring: Real-time data analytics and visualization using a police dashboard.
  • Programming Languages: Python, JavaScript.
  • APIs: Google Maps API, Twilio API for communication

Outcome

  • Enhanced Safety: Real-time alerts and crime monitoring have improved response times, empowering women to travel safely.
  • Effective Assistance: Successfully provided innovative solutions for discreet and effective emergency handling in various real-world scenarios.
  • Technology Integration: Seamlessly incorporated advanced features like fake shutdown and voice-activated SOS into a user-friendly application.

Awards & Recognition

  • 3rd Rank: Achieved at the National Level Hackathon for developing Saheli, showcasing its innovation and societal impact.
  • 2nd Rank: Secured at the State Level Hackathon for addressing critical women’s safety concerns with cutting-edge technology.
